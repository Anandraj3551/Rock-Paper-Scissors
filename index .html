Game Rules

How to Play Rock, Paper, Scissors:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Choose your move by clicking on the corresponding icon
  • The computer will randomly select its move
  • The winner of each round scores a point
  • First to reach the target score wins the game
  • In case of a tie, no points are awarded
